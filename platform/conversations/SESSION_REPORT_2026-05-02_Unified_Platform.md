# Solar Strive Platform Unification Session
**Date:** May 2, 2026
**Session Focus:** Removing installer network directory - consolidating prospecting, evaluation, and sales under single roof
**Status:** ✅ Complete - Platform architecture unified

---

## 🎯 Session Objective

Transform Solar Strive from a marketplace model with external installers into a vertically-integrated solar company that owns the entire customer journey from lead generation through installation completion.

---

## 🔄 Key Architecture Changes

### From: Decentralized Marketplace Model
- Material Owners funding campaigns
- External Powur contractor network
- Third-party installer directory
- Multiple stakeholder payments
- Fragmented customer experience

### To: Unified Solar Company Model
- Internal sales team managing leads
- In-house evaluation and design team
- Company-employed installation crews
- Direct employee compensation
- Single-point customer accountability

---

## 👥 Revised User Roles

### Previous Roles (Marketplace)
1. Material Owners → Fund campaigns
2. Placers → Install materials
3. Verifiers → Quality checks
4. Location Scouts → Find opportunities
5. Location Owners → Provide space

### New Roles (Integrated Company)
1. **Sales Representatives** → Manage entire sales pipeline
2. **Field Marketers** → Generate leads through material placement
3. **Solar Evaluators** → Conduct site surveys and system design
4. **Installation Teams** → Complete solar installations in-house
5. **Customers** → Single relationship from lead to installation
